SOAJS Enterprise

Transforming from a traditional environment to a digital organization requires lots of IT plumbing (Undifferentiated heavy lifting - Hybrid integration, API management and lifecycle, Multi cloud management and orchestration, Fast delivery, Analytics and Operating, & Adaptable framework) that requires time and talents preventing enterprises from achieving agility and speed. Unfortunately nowadays with all the tools in the market, there isn’t a solution that allows you to write your code and push a button to be up and running. Lots of plumbing is required, and it gets even more complicated after your first release.

SOAJS Enterprise is a cloud based enterprise Adaptive Development and Integration Platform (ADIP) that allows you to build, integrate and manage applications quickly and cost-effectively on any cloud without compromising security, scalability, and reliability – while staying adaptive to any architecture, deployment technology, or legacy systems. It is the richest and most complete in many harmonized features covering the main pillars needed to achieve agility such as:

  • Comprehensive API lifecycle management and gateway
  • API Builder, catalog & test simulator
  • Multi cloud orchestration & distributed architecture
  • Continuous integration and continuous delivery
  • Catalogue deployment and smart release manager
  • Deploy from source code with framework freedom (php, java, nodejs, etc.)
  • Bridge to your existing systems and applications
  • Resource management, integration, and configuration
  • Complete analytic and operating metrics
 
 

With this solution, organizations become lean & mean, accelerate time to market and guarantee quality, reusability, and reliability of modern application designs – without having to re-architect and re-platform existing applications to your successful digital transformation.

About

We Understand your Pain

Innovation, Time-to-Market, Standardization, Speed, Speed, Speed and Fail Fast – these are just a few of the terms that you will hear from us.

Our mission is to empower enterprises with the digital business agility required to respond quickly and effectively to emerging threats to their business, and proactively seize new market opportunities.

Herron Tech has leveraged the SOAJS Open Source Platform to provide an array of turnkey software products that are designed to significantly increase stability, standardization and ROI, while decreasing valuable time-to-market.

A couple years ago, we realized the majority of organizations, even the one we were part of, all had the same common problems. They had to innovate and adapt quickly to many changes in their market. In 2015 we went on a mission to build a smart solution to help enterprises of all sizes to achieve digital transformation in a quick and efficient way.

As a result we launched SOAJS, an open source application platform initiative built by engineers for engineers in a tactical way for rapid execution at all stages of the digital transformation. SOAJS was created by the founders of Herron Tech and is now supported by a global community.

SOAJS is the only complete Enterprise Open Source aPaaS to build auto-scaling SOA based solutions to achieve digital transformation efficiently and seamlessly. SOAJS is maintained by top-notch engineers around the globe as an Open Source community today with the following values: Fast, Free, & Flexible.

To make sure we are steering this initiative into the right direction, we worked closely with different enterprises from all sizes (from startups to billion dollar publicly traded enterprises) to help solve this complex problem in a versatile way. We can proudly say that we found the right solution that can be installed locally or on any cloud in just minutes.
